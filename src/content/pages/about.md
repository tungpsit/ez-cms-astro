# About EZ CMS

EZ CMS is a modern content management system designed for developers and content creators who want the best of both worlds: the performance of static sites and the flexibility of dynamic CMS platforms.

## Our Mission

We believe content management should be:

- **Simple** - Focus on writing, not fighting with tools
- **Fast** - Your readers deserve instant page loads
- **Flexible** - Customize everything to fit your needs
- **Open** - Built on open standards and open source

## Technology Stack

EZ CMS is built with:

- **Astro** - The web framework for content-driven websites
- **React** - For interactive admin components
- **Tailwind CSS** - For beautiful, responsive design
- **TypeScript** - For type-safe development

## Get Involved

EZ CMS is open source. Contribute on GitHub, report issues, or suggest features. Together, we're building the future of content management.
